Who are we?

At Pet Expo, we're more than just an event – we're a vibrant celebration of the special bond between humans and animals. Our dedicated team of pet lovers and industry professionals curate unforgettable experiences for pets and their owners alike. We showcase the latest innovations in pet care, from premium nutrition to cutting-edge technology, while fostering education and awareness about responsible pet ownership. With interactive exhibits, engaging seminars, and fun-filled activities, Pet Expo promises an immersive experience for all attendees, whether they have furry, feathered, or scaly companions. Join us in celebrating the joy and companionship that pets bring to our lives at Pet Expo – where the love for animals knows no bounds.

Our Mission

At Pet Expo, our mission is simple yet profound: to foster a community where pets are celebrated, cherished, and understood. Through our events, we aim to: Educate: We provide valuable information and resources on responsible pet ownership, animal welfare, and pet care practices. Connect: We bring together pet enthusiasts, experts, and businesses to share knowledge, experiences, and the latest innovations in the pet industry. Inspire: We inspire people to deepen their connection with their pets, explore new activities, and create lifelong memories together.

FOUNDER

Emily Rodriguez

Emily Rodriguez, the founder of Pet Expo, channels her lifelong passion for animals into a vibrant celebration of the human-animal bond. Raised on a Texas farm, Emily's vision for Pet Expo emerged from her desire to create a community where pet lovers could unite and thrive. With determination and compassion, she overcame obstacles to establish Pet Expo as a beloved institution. Emily's commitment extends beyond business; she actively supports animal welfare causes and advocates for responsible pet ownership. Through Pet Expo, Emily continues to enrich the lives of pets and their owners, embodying the transformative power of the human-animal connection.
